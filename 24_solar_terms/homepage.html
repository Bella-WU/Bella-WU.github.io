The  24  Solar  Terms

According to the solar term system, each year is divided into 24 segments. Most segments last 15 days and are meant to capture variations in season and other climatic phenomenon. Every term has different meaning, though the date may vary in the Gregorian calendar.

1 Year = 4 Seasons = 12 Months = 24 Terms
